About Us

A community rooted in faith and love.

Our Vision

Disciples. Leaders. Reached people groups.

Bethel Full Gospel Church is a vibrant community of believers dedicated to spreading the love of Christ. Founded in 2006, our church has been a beacon of hope and faith in the community.

We create a welcoming environment where individuals and families can grow in their faith, build meaningful relationships, and serve the community.

Koratagere Ministry

At Koratagere Ministries, Brother Manju is leading all the believers to gather in worship, pray, and grow in their faith. Rooted in love and unity, we seek to encounter God's presence and share His hope with others. Our vision is to make disciples, raise up leaders, and reach unreached people groups.
